My Hobbies:)

This was first, because I loved sports, but also because I was 15 years old and 6 feet tall. Long story short, I chose basketball. When I started studying at LCC International University, I got the chance to play on a profesional womens basketball team, named "Fortuna". Although I am not playing on that team now, I will always love basketball (and sports) and will always find time to play! (I even train girls weekly at LCC, who want to learn how to play.)
